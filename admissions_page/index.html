Your Future Starts with Admissions

To get started, let’s take a quick look at the admissions process for a Coding Dojo bootcamp.

Do Your Research!

  • Check our our programs here on our website and download a sylabus to view the curriculum
  • Schedule a call with one of our admissions advisors to compare courses or discuss financing.
  • Attend an open house to hear directly from instructors and career services advisors.

Submit Application

  • Next, submit your application! The application process takes no more than 5 minutes.
  • If applying for a full-time program, schedule your interview to complete your application
  • Receive your decision immediately for part-time programs or within 2-3 business days for full-time programs.

Get Financing For Your Program

  • Our admissions advisors can help you decide what is best for you based on your financial situation and your goals.
  • We offer a variety of payment options, including partial scholarships, installment plans, and third party lenders including Ascent and Climb Credit.

Finalize Your Enrollment

  • Once you have confirmed your financing, submit your deposit* to reserve your seat in class
  • *Deposit may be waived for certain financing options
  • Connect with your Onboarding Manager! Your manager will help you gather all your needed information and get you set up in the learn platform.

Start Your Learning Journey

Get signed into your tools, complete your pre-course work, meet your classmates, and get excited for the first day of class. You’re on your way to a brand new career in tech!

Schedule a Call with the Admissions Team!

  • Get course recommendations
  • Learn all our course options
  • Discover custom financing options
  • Get all your questions answered

Financing Overview

  • Pay in Full

    Save $250

    Pay your tuition in full upon enrollment to save $250 off the total cost of your program. Tuition is fully refundable up to the first day of class.

  • Pay in Installments

    Divide your tuition payments over the length of your program. These installment plans carry 0% interest, and you can adjust your payment schedule at any time.

  • Third Party Funding

    Cover up to 100% of your tuition and defer your payments until 3 months after you graduate. There is no hard credit pull to see what you qualify for.
